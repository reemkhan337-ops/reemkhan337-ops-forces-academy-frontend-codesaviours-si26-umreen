Admissions 2024

How to apply, eligibility criteria, required documents and fee structure.

Simple Process

How to Apply

Follow these four easy steps to get enrolled at Forces Academy.

  1. 1
    Visit the Academy

    Come to our Faisalabad or Rawalpindi campus and talk to our admissions counsellor.

  2. 2
    Pick Your Course

    Choose the course that matches your career goal and schedule preference.

  3. 3
    Submit Documents

    Bring the required documents listed below and fill in the admission form.

  4. 4
    Pay and Start

    Pay the first month's fee and you are all set. Classes begin the next day.

📄 Required Documents

Bring original documents along with 2 photocopies of each.

  • ✔ Original CNIC / B-Form of student
  • ✔ Parent/Guardian CNIC (photocopy)
  • ✔ Latest academic result card / degree
  • ✔ School/College Leaving Certificate
  • ✔ 4 recent passport-size photographs
  • ✔ Domicile Certificate (photocopy)
  • ✔ Character Certificate from last institution
  • ✔ Medical Certificate (fit for training)
