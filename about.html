About Forces Academy

Our history, mission, vision, and the team behind Pakistan's finest academy.

Our Story

A Legacy of Excellence

Forces Academy was founded in 1998 by retired Brigadier Tariq Mahmood with a single goal: to provide world-class preparation to Pakistani youth aspiring to serve in the Armed Forces and Civil Services.

What started as a small coaching centre with 30 students in Faisalabad has grown into a nationally recognised institution with campuses across Pakistan and thousands of successful alumni serving at the highest levels of the military and government.

Our approach combines rigorous academic coaching with physical training, psychological preparation, and personality development — everything a candidate needs to succeed.

1998
Academy Founded

Started with 30 students in a small centre in Susan Road, Faisalabad.

2003
First Major Expansion

Moved to a larger campus and added CSS/PMS preparation courses.

2010
Rawalpindi Campus Opened

Second campus launched to serve students in the twin cities.

2018
Online Learning Launched

Full Stack LMS introduced for students across Pakistan and abroad.

2024
25 Years of Excellence

Over 12,500 successful alumni and still growing strong.

Mission & Vision

🎯 Our Mission

To provide Pakistan's youth with the highest quality preparation for Armed Forces and Civil Services through expert coaching, disciplined training, and character development.

  • Deliver exam-focused, results-driven instruction
  • Develop confident, disciplined, and capable individuals
  • Make quality defence preparation accessible to all
  • Maintain a 98%+ success rate year after year
💡 Our Vision

To become South Asia's most respected and trusted academy for defence and civil services preparation — one that every Pakistani family trusts with their child's future.

  • Expand to all major cities of Pakistan by 2030
  • Produce 1,000+ successful officers every year
  • Build a world-class online learning platform
  • Partner with leading universities and institutions
